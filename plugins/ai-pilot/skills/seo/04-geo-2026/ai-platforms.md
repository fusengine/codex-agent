## Platform Characteristics

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- **Strengths**: Conversational, creative, code generation
- **Citation Style**: Footnotes with links
- **Content Preference**: Detailed, structured, recent
- **Optimization**: Clear definitions, examples, step-by-step

### Google Gemini
- **Strengths**: Multimodal (text + images), Google ecosystem
- **Citation Style**: Inline references with Google Search integration
- **Content Preference**: Authoritative sources, E-E-A-T signals
- **Optimization**: Schema markup, Google-indexed content

### Perplexity AI
- **Strengths**: Research-oriented, academic citations
- **Citation Style**: Numbered references with URLs
- **Content Preference**: Data-driven, statistics, recent sources
- **Optimization**: Citations to authoritative sources, data points

### Microsoft Copilot
- **Strengths**: Bing integration, enterprise features
- **Citation Style**: Links in responses
- **Content Preference**: Microsoft ecosystem, professional content
- **Optimization**: LinkedIn profiles, business content

### Codex (Anthropic)
- **Strengths**: Safety-focused, nuanced understanding
- **Citation Style**: Contextual attribution
- **Content Preference**: Ethical, balanced, accurate
- **Optimization**: Transparency, multiple perspectives

---

## Cross-Platform Optimization

### Universal Best Practices

1. **Clear Structure**: H1-H6 hierarchy
2. **Direct Answers**: First 100 words
3. **Citations**: Link to authoritative sources
4. **Data Points**: Statistics with sources
5. **Expert Quotes**: Attributed opinions
6. **Recency**: Update dates visible
7. **E-E-A-T**: Author credentials, transparency
