# Google Business Profile Optimization (2026)

**Complete guide to optimize your Google Business Profile listing.**

## Why GBP is Critical in 2026

- **32-35%** of local ranking signal weight
- Integration with **AI Overviews** and **Gemini**
- Primary source for **Local Pack** (3-pack)
- 76% of "near me" searches visit a business within 24 hours

---

## Complete Optimization Checklist

### 1. Basic Information (NAP)

```text
✅ Exact business name (no keyword stuffing)
✅ Complete verified address
✅ Local phone number (not 800 numbers)
✅ Website with UTM tracking
✅ Regular AND special hours (holidays)
```

### 2. Categories (CRITICAL)

```text
✅ Primary category = CORE business activity
✅ Up to 9 relevant secondary categories
✅ Check well-ranking competitors' categories
```

**Example:**
- Primary: `Italian Restaurant`
- Secondary: `Pizzeria`, `Caterer`, `Delivery Service`

### 3. Optimized Description

```markdown
Recommended structure (750 characters max):
1. Hook sentence with main keyword + city
2. Key services/products
3. Differentiators (experience, certifications)
4. Call-to-action
```

**Example:**
> Plumber in Paris 15th for 25 years, ABC Plumbing provides 24/7 emergency service for repairs, installation, and renovation. RGE certified, free quote within 2h. Call now!

### 4. Attributes (Often Neglected)

```text
✅ Accessibility (wheelchair, parking, etc.)
✅ Service options (dine-in, takeout, delivery)
✅ Accepted payment methods
✅ "Women-owned" / "Small business"
✅ Wi-Fi, outdoor seating, AC...
```

## Active Content (Freshness Signals)

### Google Posts (1-3x per week)

| Type | Usage | Duration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Update** | News, announcements | 7 days |
| **Event** | Events with dates | Until date |
| **Offer** | Promotions with CTA | Customizable |

**Optimal format:**
- Image: 1200x900px (4:3 ratio)
- Text: 150-300 words
- Clear CTA: "Book", "Learn more", "Call"

### Photos and Videos (Weekly)

```text
✅ GEOTAGGED photos (EXIF with GPS coordinates)
✅ Minimum 100 photos for maximum impact
✅ Variety: storefront, interior, team, products, customers
✅ Videos <30 seconds
✅ File naming: "plumber-paris-15-service.jpg"
```

**Measured impact:** +35% engagement with geotagged photos

### Q&A Section (Proactive)

```text
✅ Pre-fill with 10-15 frequent questions
✅ Answer your own questions
✅ Include keywords naturally in answers
✅ Monitor new questions (respond <24h)
```

## Mistakes to Avoid

```text
❌ Business name with keyword stuffing
❌ Virtual address or PO box
❌ Primary category too generic
❌ Stock photos (detected by Google)
❌ Outdated hours
❌ Ignoring negative reviews
❌ Overly promotional posts without value
```
